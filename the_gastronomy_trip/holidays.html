Holidays

Every Country has holydays. Every country has a story to tell. Come and see the magic that each one of them has to show you!

Our Values

Every culture is different, and so are its holidays. If there’s one aspect that all of them share is the fact that they deserve respect, despite of what you may dislike about it. In our community, we believe that in order to have the best experience here as well as live a happy life, we must spread respect and love for all kind of people and embrace our differences. You may find a recipe with ingredients that you would never think about combining, “who eats that?!” you may exclaim, but there’s people out there that may have cultural, environmental, and even religious reasons for eating, doing, and believing the way they live, so be kind in your comments when talking about other cultures, even if you find them hard to understand.

Ideas

This Place is for you and your family, friends, and loved ones, to experiment with the infinite ways to have fun, while at the same time understanding the dynamic and history of the cultures of the world. You may find interesting to make a thematic party and invite everyone; you may choose one country and play movies from that land, or reproduce traditional music while enjoying traditional meals from your chosen country.
